# Positive Vibes: Daily affirmations to brighten your day 
**Privacy Policy**
  **Last updated:** December 26, 2024

  **Alpha Cat Studios** built the Positive Vibes app as a Free app supported by ads.

  ## Information Collection and Use

  The app collects minimal data to function:

  ### Local Storage
  - Theme preferences
  - Favorite affirmations
  - Notification settings
  - Daily streak count

  This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

  ### Advertising
  We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:
  - Device identifiers
  - IP address
  - Usage data

  For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

  ## Third-Party Services

  The app uses these third-party services:
  - **Google AdMob** - For displaying advertisements
  - **Google Play Billing** - For in-app purchases (Google Play only)

  ## Children's Privacy

  This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
